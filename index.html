Solar Harvest Energy

Integrated photovoltaic cells convert ambient and direct light into clean energy — enough to run the entire ecosystem on just a few hours of sunlight.

🧠

AI Growth Engine

Twelve embedded sensors feed real-time data to an onboard neural model that autonomously adjusts lighting spectrum, nutrient dosing, and humidity cycles.

💧

Closed-Loop Hydroponics

A recirculating water system uses up to 95% less water than conventional soil farming. No dirt. No mess. Just thriving roots and clean produce.

📱

Companion App

Monitor crop health, get harvest notifications, and swap grow profiles — all from your phone. The app speaks plant so you don't have to.

🌡️

Climate Control

Micro-climate regulation maintains the precise temperature band each plant species needs, whether you're in a Kochi summer or a Helsinki winter.

♻️

Zero Packaging

The bag is the product. Every snack you harvest replaces a single-use plastic wrapper. Over a year, that's hundreds of packets kept out of landfills.

Process

From pocket to
plate in days.

1
Choose your crop
Open the app, browse 20+ supported varieties — from strawberries and basil to cherry tomatoes — and select your grow profile. Hive downloads the exact protocol.
2
Drop in the seed pod
Biodegradable seed capsules slot into the hydroponic chamber in seconds. Top up the water reservoir, close the lid, and you're done.
3
Carry it. Let it grow.
The AI monitors everything automatically. Solar panels top up battery as you move through your day. You just carry it like a bag.
4
Harvest and eat
Your phone notifies you when it's time. Open the chamber, pluck your snack fresh, and replant instantly. The cycle is 7–21 days depending on the crop.
